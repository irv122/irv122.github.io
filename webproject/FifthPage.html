Fifth Page

Luna's isolation were not all tears and sadness, she also was able to do self valuations and face herself and her fears. Over the months of seclution, she got to know herself and finally was able to listen to what her heart had been screaming at her for, over the years. Growing up she has always been an adventorous person, she found passion in learning different unique cultures and in meeting people with distinctive backgrounds; her biggest dream was to know and visit everything the world had to offer. She had forgotten about this huge piece of herself and somehow lost sight of it; she finally realized that this has been what had been eating at her throughhout the recent years, this was the source of her darkness that had almost inveloped her entirely, this was were it all originated from. She had finally found herself and she felt more whole then she had ever in a such a long time. One morning at her cottage, her family reached out to her. They wanted to be there for her and feared loosing her, she felt a sense of happiness to see that she was right in believing that her family cared about her; her cousin Luz was amongst them and as soon as they layed eyes on each other they immediately smiled, nodded and embraced each other. There is never hard feelings in her family, no matter how big the arguments could be. That day the family had a gathering at Luna's cottage and had a small ruinion party on the level that only a dominican family could have. Amidst the good times with her family she looked for a second at the ocean,(her confidant and friend in her darkest times.) she looked for a minute and smiled wide with watery eyes and whispered "thank you."
