The Girl Who Could

This is a short story of courage and self- discovery, is a story of one girl and the story of us all. Luna was her name, she lived in a small town in Dominican Republic, called jarabacoa. Luna was carefree and loved by her community, she had a deep rooted love and passion for her beautiful country and culture and was met with happiness and fullfillment for the majority of her life, she asked for nothing more out of life. Though Unbeknown to anyone else, she held a deep turmoil in her heart, a personal battle within herself that was between her and herself and no one was a part of or had any knowedge of.
